  <ul>
        <li><strong>User-Friendly Interface:</strong> Our distro comes with a clean and intuitive desktop environment that makes it easy to use, even for newcomers.</li>
        <li><strong>Customizability:</strong> SpoinkOS is highly customizable, allowing you to tailor your Linux experience to your liking.</li>
        <li><strong>Uses KDE:</strong> For all your usages KDE makes it better.</li>
        <li><strong>Based On Ubuntu:</strong> Comes With an Ubuntu Base</li>

    </ul>
    <h2>Installation</h2>

  <ol>
        <li>Download the ISO image from our <a href="https://archive.org/details/spoink-os">official website</a>.</li>
        <li>Create a bootable USB drive using your preferred tool (e.g., Rufus, Etcher).</li>
        <li>Boot your computer from the USB drive.</li>
        <li>Follow the on-screen instructions to install SpoinkOS.</li>
